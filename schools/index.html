How It Works

The Intelligence Layer Explained.

Traditional CCTV systems record footage that no one watches. AI agents watch every feed, every second — detecting threats, flagging anomalies, and alerting the right person before an incident escalates. Think of it less like a camera system and more like hiring a team of tireless, hyper-alert security analysts stationed at every corner of your campus.

A simple analogy: A CCTV system is a tool — it records, but needs a human to watch, interpret, and respond. An AI agent is more like an experienced school security head who monitors every entrance, corridor, and playground simultaneously, flags anomalies the moment they appear, and hands you a decision — not hours of footage. The difference isn't incremental. It's structural.
